ClearClaims for Claimants

Organize the claim without becoming overwhelmed.

For people managing their own claim, ClearClaims keeps the timeline, policy questions, documents, and next steps in one guided workspace.

BenefitsClaim timeline, document uploads, policy explanations, weekly next-step prompts.
DifferentBuilt around the claim record, not generic file storage or a simple task app.

ClearClaims for Contractors

Manage active claim jobs without seeing private claim strategy.

For contractors, ClearClaims keeps estimates, invoices, submissions, reports, and waiting-on status tied to active jobs/claims.

BenefitsAssigned jobs, estimate and invoice uploads, contractor communications, submission history.
DifferentContractors see the work they are part of, not PA-customer or PA-adjuster private communications.

ClearClaims for Public Adjusters

Run every claim from the complete record.

For PAs, ClearClaims gives the highest access level for claim workflow, evidence, linked claimant access, contractor coordination, and claim history.

BenefitsClaim portfolio, linked claimant and contractor activity, communications, evidence, and reporting.
DifferentPA-managed claimants get included access, so the claim team works from one shared source of truth.
